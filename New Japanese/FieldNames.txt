Unique  --  A unique combination of the word and it's reading (e.g. "床[ゆか]")
Word  --  The word as normally written (e.g. "床")
Reading  --  The reading (e.g. "ゆか")
English -- An English translation (e.g. "floor")
Sound -- A sound file pronouncing the word (e.g. "[sound:yomichan_audio_ゆか_床_2022-02-19-15-32-35.mp3]")


I'd recommend to fill these fields using Yomichan, with the following card format:

Unique: {expression}[{reading}]
Word: {expression}
Reading: {reading}
English: {glossary-brief}
Sound: {audio}
